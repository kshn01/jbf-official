About us

Crafting Quality Furniture since
1998

Jay Bhavani Furniture has been a trusted name in Navsari for over 28 years. We pride ourselves on custom manufacturing and delivering premium living room, bedroom, dining, and office furniture with exceptional local craftsmanship.

Our Values

Every piece we build reflects decades of dedication to the families we serve.

  • 1

    Customer First

    We listen to your needs and work with you to create furniture that truly fits your home and your life.

  • 2

    Uncompromising Quality

    From the materials we select to the finishing touches, quality is our non-negotiable standard at every step.

  • 3

    Community Roots

    We are proud to be a local Navsari business, employing skilled local artisans and serving our neighbours for generations.

Why Choose Jay Bhavani Furniture?

We believe in blending traditional craftsmanship with modern design to deliver the perfect furniture for your home.

Local Manufacturing

All our furniture is crafted locally by skilled artisans, ensuring the highest quality and supporting our community.

Premium Quality

Custom Designs

We work closely with you to design and build bespoke furniture that fits perfectly in your space.

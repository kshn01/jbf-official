A successful showroom visit starts with preparation. Write down room dimensions, note the materials you prefer, and bring inspiration images so your furniture choice matches your home.

## What to bring to the showroom
Bring a smartphone photo of the room you are furnishing, the wall color, and any fabrics or tiles you want to match. This helps the showroom team recommend the right furniture style.

## Ask about delivery and installation
Local furniture showrooms in Gujarat often provide delivery and installation. Confirm the lead time, installation fees, and whether the showroom offers assembly services.

## Eco-friendly and practical choices
Ask about sustainable material options, such as responsibly sourced wood and durable upholstery. Practical finishes help your furniture remain beautiful for years.
